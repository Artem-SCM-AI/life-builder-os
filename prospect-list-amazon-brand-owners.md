Before adding anyone to your CRM, verify 2+ of these:

☐ Title includes: Founder, Owner, CEO, Brand Owner, Head of Operations

☐ Headline or experience mentions: Amazon, FBA, private label, e-commerce brand

☐ Company appears to sell physical products (not software, not an agency)

☐ Company has 1–50 employees (individual brand, not a conglomerate)

☐ Based in US, Canada, UK, or Australia

☐ Profile shows 2+ years in Amazon / e-commerce

Disqualify if:

They work for Amazon.com directly

They are a consultant, coach, or agency (selling services, not products)

Company is a wholesale distributor (not private label)

Revenue appears to be under $100K/year (too small for $49/month)
