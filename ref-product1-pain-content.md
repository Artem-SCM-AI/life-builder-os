# Product 1 — Pain Content Library (English, US Audience)

Target: American professionals. Tone: direct, conversational, results-focused.

## Data Structure per Pain Block

- `hoursWasted`: hours/week lost currently
- `hoursWithAI`: hours/week after AI setup
- `routine`: framing when tagged "routine"
- `hate`: framing when tagged "hate"
- `steps`: 3 AI solution steps
- `result`: Before → After line
- `diy`: tool + setup time + cost
- `buy`: market rate for done-for-you

## Time Calculator Logic

When user selects pains, sum their `hoursWasted` values.
Sum their `hoursWithAI` values.

Display:
"You're spending **X hours/week** on tasks that AI can handle.
With the right setup: **Y hours/week**.
That's **Z hours back** — every single week."

Where:
- X = sum of hoursWasted for selected pains
- Y = sum of hoursWithAI for selected pains  
- Z = X - Y

Example: user selects 5 pains with hoursWasted of [2, 1.5, 4.5, 3, 8] = 19 hrs/week
With AI: [0.1, 0, 0.5, 0.2, 2] = 2.8 hrs/week
Result: "You're spending 19 hours/week. With AI: 2.8 hours. That's 16 hours back — every week."
