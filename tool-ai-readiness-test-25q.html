Is Your Business Ready for AI?

25-question assessment · ~8 minutes

Most companies that failed their AI project believed they were ready. They weren't wrong to try — they were wrong about where they actually stood.

The gap between how we see our operations and how they actually function is real, and it's almost always bigger than we expect. This is not a character flaw. It's a structural problem: we're inside the system, so we see the intent, not the gaps.

This assessment is a diagnostic tool — not a verdict, not a ranking, not a judgment. Its only job is to give you an accurate picture of where you are today, so whatever decision you make next is based on reality, not optimism.

Answer as things are right now. Not how they were six months ago, not how they'll be after the next hire, not how you'd like them to be. The result is only as useful as your honesty.
