## 2. The Problem

E-commerce brands doing $2M–$20M/year have a supply chain that "works" — until it doesn't.

The losses are real and measurable:

**Supplier overcharges** — price bumps hidden in email threads, wrong quantities on invoices. Real case: $2,914 added on top of a $47K order. Paid. Caught retroactively.

**3PL invoice errors** — wrong shipping zones, incorrect surcharges, storage billed that wasn't used. Real case: $91,800 recovered over 8 months at one brand.

**Stockouts without warning** — demand spikes above plan, nobody notices for 10 days, the SKU is gone. Season over.

**Demurrage from delayed shipments** — nobody tracks free time at the port. Real case: $1,400 from an 8-day delay, discovered on the invoice.

**Cash flow strain** — too much capital locked in inventory, payment terms misaligned with cash cycles, order frequency wrong.

These aren't rare events. They happen monthly, at brands that believe their supply chain is fine.

The root cause: the data is there. The logic to act on it isn't.

## 3. What SCAIT Is

**SCAIT** — Supply Chain AI Technology — is a supply chain consulting agency for e-commerce brands doing $2M–$20M/year in the EU and USA.

The framework: **Signals → Rules → Decisions**

Every supply chain produces signals — stock levels, invoice discrepancies, supplier delays, cash flow pressure. Most brands see these signals. Very few have the rules (agreed logic) to act on them fast and consistently. So decisions happen late, manually, and reactively.

SCAIT's job: surface the signals, define the rules, build the systems that execute the decisions — before the signals turn into losses.
