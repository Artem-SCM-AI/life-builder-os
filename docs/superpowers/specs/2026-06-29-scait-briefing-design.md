## 2. The Problem

E-commerce brands doing $2M–$20M/year have a supply chain that "works" — until it suddenly doesn't.

The hidden losses are real and measurable:

- **Supplier overcharges** that pass through undetected. Real case: $2,914 added on top of a $47K invoice. The client found out from us — not from their own review.
- **3PL invoice errors** — wrong zones, incorrect surcharges, billing for storage that wasn't used. Real case: $91,800 recovered over 8 months at a single brand.
- **Stockouts without warning** — demand spikes above plan, nobody notices for 10 days, the SKU is gone and the season is over.
- **Demurrage from delayed shipments** — not flagged until the invoice arrives. Real case: $1,400 from an 8-day delay.
- **Cash flow strain from poor SC design** — too much capital locked in inventory, payment terms misaligned with cash cycles, order frequency too high or too low.

These aren't rare events. They happen monthly, at brands that consider their SC "under control."

The root cause is always the same: the business has **signals** (data exists) but no **rules** (no agreed logic to act on it) — so decisions come late, manually, and reactively.

## 4. What SCAIT Is

**SCAIT** is a supply chain consulting agency for e-commerce brands doing $2M–$20M/year in the EU and USA.

The methodology is built on three layers:

**Signals → Rules → Decisions**

| Layer | What It Means |
|-------|--------------|
| **Signals** | What's happening in your SC right now — stock levels, invoice discrepancies, supplier response patterns, cash flow stress points |
| **Rules** | The logic that should govern each signal — when to reorder, what to dispute, how to structure payment terms |
| **Decisions** | The action taken — or automated — based on that logic |

Most brands have signals (the data is there) but no rules (no agreed logic to act on it) — so decisions come too late, manually, and inconsistently.

SCAIT builds the full stack: surfaces the signals, defines the rules, executes the decisions.
