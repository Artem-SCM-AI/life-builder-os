## Gumroad Copy (final)

**Tagline:**
> Stop guessing whether you need AI or automation. Answer 25 questions — get a clear verdict.

---

**Description:**

You're about to invest 3 months and real budget into the wrong thing.

Most people pick AI or automation based on hype, not analysis. AI is more visible — so AI wins by default. Or someone saw a Zapier demo — so automation wins. Either way, the wrong choice costs you 6 months of work and a burned budget to fix.

**This test gives you the right answer — before you build anything.**

**How it works**

Think of one specific workflow in your job. Answer 25 statements about it on a 0–4 scale. Takes 3–5 minutes. You get a score from 0 to 100 — and a clear verdict.

**The 4 verdicts**

| Score | Verdict | What it means |
|-------|---------|---------------|
| 76–100 | Classic Automation | Use Zapier, Make, Power Automate. No AI needed — it would only add cost and complexity. |
| 56–75 | Automation-First | Core is automatable. Add AI only for edge cases and exceptions. |
| 36–55 | Intelligent Automation | You need both working together. AI handles judgment — automation handles execution. |
| 0–35 | AI-First | Too dynamic for classic automation. You need an LLM or AI agent. |

**Each verdict includes** specific tools, free learning resources, and time estimates to build — so you leave with a clear next step, not just a label.

| | |
|---|---|
| **3–5 min** | To complete |
| **25** | Questions |
| **4** | Possible verdicts with clear guidance |
| **0** | Tech skills needed |

**Who built this**

My name is Artem. I spent 5 years running supply chains for a $25M Amazon brand. In that time I made — and fixed — exactly this mistake: deploying AI where we needed automation, and automation where we needed AI.

I built this test to give you the analysis before the investment, not after.
