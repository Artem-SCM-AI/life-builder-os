## Column Structure

| Date | Day | Week | Type | Topic | Views | 👍 | 🔥 | Notes |
|------|-----|------|------|-------|-------|----|-----|-------|

**Column definitions:**
- **Date**: Post publication date (YYYY-MM-DD)
- **Day**: Day of week (Mon, Tue, Wed, Thu, Fri, Sat, Sun)
- **Week**: Week number in the campaign (1–8)
- **Type**: Post category — "Main" (long-form) or "Short" (weekly observation)
- **Topic**: Post title/subject in Ukrainian
- **Views**: Number of post views (filled after posting, checked ~24h later)
- **👍**: Number of emoji reactions (default: 👍)
- **🔥**: Number of 🔥 reactions
- **Notes**: Optional — engagement patterns, issues, A/B notes

## How to Use

1. **Create the Google Sheet** manually with the column headers (row 1) listed above.
2. **Copy the Sheets URL** and paste it into the placeholder at the top of this file.
3. **Add all rows** from the schedule above to your sheet.
4. **Update Views & Reactions** approximately 24 hours after each post publishes:
   - Refresh your Telegram channel
   - Copy view count → paste into **Views** column
   - Copy 👍 and 🔥 reaction counts → paste into respective columns
5. **Review weekly** during your weekly session to identify patterns and adjust topics if needed.
