## Description (paste into Gumroad description field)

**Job searching is a full-time job — and you probably already have one.**

You check LinkedIn, see the same 20 postings, apply to a few, lose track of which ones, check again tomorrow. Good roles come and go in 48 hours. You're applying too late, following up inconsistently, and burning 1–2 hours a day just looking.

---

**What if your job search ran itself every morning?**

This is an autonomous AI agent that searches for jobs, builds your pipeline, and updates it daily — while you sleep.

No subscription. No SaaS. You own it. It runs on your Mac using Claude Code.

---

**Here's what it does:**

**Step 0 — Setup (one time, ~30 minutes)**
You define your ICP: target role, location preference (remote/hybrid/on-site), seniority level, target industries. Connect your Notion workspace.

**Step 1 — Deep search (first run)**
Scans all relevant postings from the last 30 days. Analyzes each one: relevance, company notes, red flags. Builds your full Notion pipeline — every role, organized, with status tracking from day one.

**Step 2 — Daily search (every morning, automatic)**
Finds new postings matching your ICP. Updates your Notion pipeline. Archives stale listings. You wake up to a fresh, ranked list. You decide which ones to pursue — the agent does everything else.

**Step 3 — Resume template**
A structured template that adapts to each specific role through Claude. One template, every application, always relevant.

**Step 4 — Motivational message**
A personalized note generated from your profile and situation. Because the search gets hard sometimes, and a good word helps.
