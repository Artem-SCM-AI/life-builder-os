## Change 1 — Section 1: Credit Facility (rewritten)

**Original:**
> Party A hereby grants Party B a revolving credit facility exclusively for the shipment in accordance with confirmed orders. This credit facility is subject to review, adjustment, and conditions as specified herein.

**Proposed:**
> Party A hereby grants Party B a credit facility on a per-invoice basis, covering shipping fees and service fees for each individual shipment in accordance with confirmed orders. No fixed aggregate revolving credit limit applies. Each invoice issued by Party A constitutes a discrete credit event under this Agreement. This credit facility is subject to the conditions as specified herein.

**Reason:** No aggregate revolving credit line exists. Credit is extended per individual invoice only.

## Change 3 — Section 12.1: Entire Agreement (scope clarified)

**Original:**
> This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, or agreements.

**Proposed:**
> This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties **with respect to the subject matter hereof** and supersedes all prior negotiations, representations, or agreements **relating specifically to the credit facility governed herein. It does not supersede or replace any separate service, logistics, or fulfillment agreement between the parties.**

**Reason:** Prevents this agreement from inadvertently voiding existing service/logistics agreements.

## Change 4 — New Section 12.6: Business Scope Limitation (new clause)

**Proposed (new):**
> **12.6 Business Scope Limitation:** This Agreement is entered into solely by and on behalf of Party B as an independent legal entity. It shall not be construed to extend to, bind, create obligations for, or otherwise affect any other business, company, venture, or personal enterprise owned, co-owned, or otherwise associated with any shareholder, director, or officer of Party B in their personal or other business capacities. The rights and obligations herein are exclusively those of Party B as a legal entity.

**Reason:** Ensures that no owner or co-owner of Party B bears personal liability or obligation under this Agreement in connection with any business other than Party B.
