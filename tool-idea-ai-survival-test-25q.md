## How This Works

Think about the core of your business idea — what you're selling and to whom.
Answer each question on a scale from 0 to 4.

**Scoring scale:**
- **0** — Not true at all
- **1** — Slightly true

**Higher score = more protected from AI disruption.**
**Lower score = higher vulnerability.**

## The Two Strategic Moves That Matter

**1. Make AI your employee, not your competitor.**
The businesses that will thrive aren't the ones AI can't touch — they're the ones that use AI to do more with less. Find what AI does well and delegate it. Keep what only you can do.

**2. Build the moat that AI cannot cross.**
Trust. Relationships. Physical presence. Local knowledge. Accountability. These are not weaknesses — they are the most durable competitive advantages in an AI-saturated market.
