## Key Insight

**The aha moment IS the entire conversion strategy.** When a prospect uploads a real invoice and sees a real overcharge flagged — they convert. All GTM effort should be designed to get prospects to that moment as fast as possible.

- Demo before signup
- "Upload your own invoice" CTA in content
- 14-day trial, no credit card required
- Trial email sequence focused on getting first invoice uploaded within 24h

## Content Strategy — Three Pillars

**Pillar 1 (40%) — Proof of problem**
Real billing errors, dollar amounts, line-item teardowns.
Hooks that work:
- "I found $4,200 in overcharges on a single 3PL invoice"
- "If you've never audited your 3PL invoices, you're almost certainly overpaying"
- "Here's a real container unload invoice with 3 billing errors — can you spot them?"

**Pillar 2 (35%) — Proof of expertise**
SC frameworks, negotiation tactics, cost reduction methodology.
Serves dual purpose: attracts job leads + product buyers. Builds authority independently of product.

**Pillar 3 (25%) — Proof of tool**
Demos embedded in problem-first framing, pilot customer results, behind-the-scenes of how AI validates invoices.

**Do NOT produce:** Feature announcements, generic logistics advice without specific numbers, testimonial-heavy posts before 10+ customers.

## Sales Motion

Problem → Demo → Trial → Paid. **No freemium.**

1. Demo first: "Can I show you something in your invoice? 5 minutes." (Live for warm contacts; video for cold audience)
2. 14-day free trial, no credit card
3. Onboarding goal: first real invoice uploaded within 24 hours
4. Aha moment drives conversion — if they find an error, they pay

**Data privacy objection (will surface in every demo):** Address proactively on upload screen — "Invoices are processed in real-time and not stored." SC directors handle sensitive vendor pricing. This must be answered before they upload anything.

## Phase Structure for Roadmap

1. **Pre-Launch Validation** — 2–3 pilot customers must find real errors before public claims are made
2. **Content Engine** — LinkedIn 2x/week maintained without gaps (gaps kill algorithmic reach)
3. **Community Penetration** — 3–4 weeks credibility-building before any product mention in communities
4. **Referral + Expansion** — Activate at 20+ customers; requires personal relationships from pilot phase

**Biggest GTM risk:** Artem running parallel job search → content lapse → LinkedIn algorithm resets. Content consistency is the #1 success factor.
