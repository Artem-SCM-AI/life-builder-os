**PHASE2-EXECUTION-GUIDE.md** — Week-by-week execution plan for 40 interviews:

- Section 1: Phase 2 goal in one line
  "Run 40 interviews (10 per product) across Products 0, EMS, DEVMON, SINV, and 3PLV. Close at least 1 deal per product. Sequence products adaptively based on interview signal."

- Section 2: Product 0 first (this week)
  - Target: 3 interviews with warm contacts using DS-SALES-SCRIPT.md
  - Lead sources: ex-colleagues, LinkedIn SC connections, any SCAI customer from Phase 1
  - Goal: 1 paying customer or LOI for Product 0 (DS-04)
  - After 3 calls: assess — if strong signal, book 7 more DS calls; if weak signal, pivot to EMS

- Section 3: Adaptive sequencing rules (how to decide next product order)
  After Product 0 launch, the order of Products EMS → DEVMON → SINV → 3PLV is determined by interview signal:

  **Strong signal criteria** (proceed with this product):
  - 3+ of first 5 prospects confirm the pain without prompting
  - At least 1 prospect says "I'd pay for this" or agrees to a follow-up demo
  - Discovery question responses are specific and detailed (not "yeah sure that's a problem")

  **Weak signal criteria** (deprioritize this product, move to next):
  - Fewer than 2 of first 5 prospects show genuine interest
  - Pain is acknowledged but described as "nice to have" rather than "this costs me money"
  - No one books a follow-up after the first 5 calls

  **Default order if signal is equal:** EMS → DEVMON → SINV → 3PLV (per D-02 decision)

- Section 4: Weekly interview cadence
  - Target: 3–5 calls per week minimum
  - Lead source split: first 2 calls warm (scripts from WARM-OUTREACH-SCRIPTS.md), remaining cold pipeline
  - After each call: immediately update INTERVIEW-SEQUENCING-TRACKER.md and Google Sheets Lead Tracker
  - Every Friday: count interviews done per product, assess signal, decide next week's product focus

- Section 5: Closing protocol
  - After demo + offer on a call:
    - If "yes" → collect payment method, send onboarding email same day, log as Closed Won
    - If "send me more info" → send a follow-up email within 2 hours with demo video + one-page summary, follow up in 3 days
    - If "LOI" → send LOI document (simple Google Doc: name, product, price, pilot period), get signed within 48 hours
    - If "no" → log outcome, ask one question: "What would have made this relevant for you?" (learning capture)

- Section 6: Phase 2 completion criteria (what "done" looks like)
  - [ ] Product 0: 3+ interviews logged, 1 Closed Won or LOI
  - [ ] EMS: 10 interviews logged, 1 Closed Won or LOI
  - [ ] DEVMON: 10 interviews logged, 1 Closed Won or LOI
  - [ ] SINV: 10 interviews logged, 1 Closed Won or LOI
  - [ ] 3PLV: 10 interviews logged, 1 Closed Won or LOI
