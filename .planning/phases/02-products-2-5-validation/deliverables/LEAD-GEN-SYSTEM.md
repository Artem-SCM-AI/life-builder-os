# Lead Generation System — Daily Playbook

**Purpose:** Systematic daily process to generate 20–30 qualified leads/day from Amazon brand owners for SCAIT Products 2–5 validation and sales.

**Time investment:** 15–20 minutes/day.

---

## Section 1: Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

**Primary target:** Amazon brand owner with an active China-sourced supply chain.

**Must-have signals (both required to qualify a lead):**
1. Sells on Amazon (FBA and/or FBM)
2. Has supply chain operations (sourcing from China, managing 3PLs, or handling inventory at scale)

**Ideal profile characteristics:**
- Company size: 1–50 employees (solopreneur to small team)
- SKU count: 5–50 active SKUs
- Revenue range: $500K–$10M/year on Amazon
- Fulfillment: FBA + 3PL combination (highest complexity = highest pain)
- Sourcing: China suppliers (freight, QC, supplier communication challenges)

**High-value buying signals (look for these in their LinkedIn activity or profile):**
- Posts or comments about supply chain pain: stockouts, 3PL issues, supplier delays, inventory problems
- Uses phrases like: "stockout", "3PL headaches", "supplier delays", "out of stock", "reorder point", "safety stock"
- Job title indicates operational responsibility (not just marketing/branding role)
- Recently posted about Amazon operations, inventory management, or sourcing

## Section 3: Daily Workflow (6-Step Execution)

Execute this sequence in 15–20 minutes each day, Monday through Friday.

1. **Open LinkedIn** — apply your saved search filters (or re-enter if expired). Sort by "Most recent" to see active profiles.

2. **Review 30–40 profiles** — quickly scan each against the ICP criteria. Qualify a lead if both must-haves are met: sells on Amazon AND has supply chain operations. Skip profiles where both are not evident within 10 seconds of scanning.

3. **Add 20–30 qualified leads to the LEAD-TRACKER Google Sheet** — for each qualified lead, enter: Full Name, LinkedIn Profile URL, Company, Title, and a brief ICP signal note (e.g., "posts about 3PL issues", "FBA brand, ~20 SKUs based on brand size"). Assign a Lead Source (Cold-LinkedIn).

4. **Send 10–15 DMs** — use scripts from WARM-OUTREACH-SCRIPTS.md for warm contacts or COLD-OUTREACH-SCRIPTS.md for cold leads. Personalize [Name] and [Company] tokens before sending. Do not copy-paste without personalizing.

5. **Log all sent messages in the tracker** — immediately after sending each DM, update the lead's row in Tab 1: set Status to "Contacted - Cold" or "Contacted - Warm", and fill in Date Contacted. Column reference: see LEAD-TRACKER-SPEC.md for full column definitions.

6. **Review replies and respond same day** — check LinkedIn notifications before closing the tab. For any reply received: respond within the same day, move the lead's Status to "Replied" if they engaged, or "Call Booked" if they agreed to a call. Log the call in Tab 2 (Interview Log) after the meeting.

### Conversion funnel

| Stage | Rate | Weekly result |
|-------|------|--------------|
| Cold reply rate | 5–10% | 3–8 replies from cold |
| Warm reply rate | 30–50% | Varies by warm list size |
| Call booking rate from replies | 40–60% | 2–4 calls booked |
| **Target minimum** | | **2–3 calls booked/week** |

### Interview target for Phase 2 (Products 2–5)

- 10 interviews per product × 4 products = 40 total interviews
- At 2–3 calls/week: 40 interviews take 13–20 weeks
- To accelerate: prioritize warm outreach (higher conversion) and request referrals after each call
