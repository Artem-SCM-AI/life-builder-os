<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| Verbal agreement on demo call → written record | Risk of misaligned expectations on what was sold |
| Invoice sent → payment received | Risk of non-payment before service starts |
| First delivery → client satisfaction | Risk of scope mismatch (client expected more) |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-04-01 | Repudiation | Client disputes what was agreed on demo call | mitigate | Send written summary email immediately after call: "Here's what we discussed: [service, price, start date]. Reply to confirm and I'll send the invoice." |
| T-04-02 | Repudiation | Client claims first delivery was inadequate | mitigate | First delivery follows SCAI-WORKFLOW.md exactly; output format shown in demo (no surprises). Day 7 check-in provides structured feedback loop. |
| T-04-03 | Denial of Service | Client provides too many supplier emails to process at $49/mo | accept | Scope cap: up to 5 active suppliers. If client has more, this is a pricing conversation (documented in SCAI-ONBOARDING-TEMPLATE.md Section 3, minute 20–25 customization discussion). |
| T-04-04 | Elevation of Privilege | Client shares their Google Sheet link with competitors | accept | Sheet contains their own data only. No cross-client data in any shared sheet. |
</threat_model>
