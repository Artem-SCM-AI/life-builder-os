**You've heard "add AI to your operations" a hundred times.**

Most of it is noise. But some of it is real — and the difference between AI that pays back fast and AI that goes nowhere is almost always the same thing: whether the business was actually ready.

Not ready doesn't mean bad operations. It means specific gaps — in data, in processes, or in ownership — that AI can't work around. Deploy on top of those gaps and you get noise. Fix them first and automation pays back inside 30 days.

**This assessment tells you exactly where you stand.**

15 questions. Three sections — Data & Visibility, Process Maturity, Team & Tools. Score each answer (0, 1, or 2). Your total score gives you one of three results:

**0–10 — Not Ready Yet**
The exact 3 gaps holding you back. Fix these before you touch any AI tool.

**11–20 — Getting There**
Which one high-ROI workflow to start with — and the verified numbers to justify it to leadership.

**21–30 — Ready to Automate**
You're leaving money on the table every month. Here's the fastest path to a measurable return this quarter.

Every result gives you a specific next step. Not "explore AI solutions." A concrete action you can take this week.
