## Description (paste into Gumroad description field)

**Most AI projects in operations fail for the same reason.**

Not the technology. The sequence.

People deploy AI before their data is clean, before the process is defined, before anyone owns the output. The project runs for 3 months. Results are vague. The team goes back to the spreadsheet — and "AI" gets written off as something that doesn't work for operations.

It works. But the sequence matters.

---

**This framework gives you a clear yes/no on whether AI is right for each of your workflows — before you spend a dollar building anything.**

**Part 1 — The 3 problems AI actually solves in e-commerce**
Pattern detection. Communication at scale. Decision support under uncertainty. If your pain fits one of these, AI will pay back fast. If it doesn't, you need a different fix.

Real numbers: $91,800 caught in 3PL invoice overcharges across 8 months. $2,914 recovered on a single $47K supplier order. Stockout flagged 14 days early.

**Part 2 — The 4 signs you're not ready for AI yet**
None of them are about the technology. All of them are fixable — if you catch them before you build. This is why most projects fail, and why most post-mortems blame the wrong thing.

**Part 3 — Decision matrix**
Rate any workflow on two dimensions: data readiness and process clarity. Your score (0–4) gives you a direct answer:
- Score 4: Start now
- Score 3: Start in 30 days (here's what to fix)
- Score 2: Start in 60–90 days
- Score 0–1: Build the foundation first — exact steps included

**Part 4 — The 5 SC workflows with the fastest ROI**
Ranked by return and deployment speed. With verified numbers. These are the workflows e-commerce brands consistently see the fastest payback on.
