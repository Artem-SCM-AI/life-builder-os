**Badge:** Layer 1 — Inventory Forecasting and Control

### AI Inventory Forecasting: Know When You'll Stock Out Before It Happens.

Most brands find out about a stockout after it happens. By then, your BSR is dropping, your PPC is wasted, and you're expediting at 3x the cost. Layer 1 gives you two AI agents that monitor your inventory and your sales plan — every 24 hours, every SKU.

**SC Stock Radar**
Every morning, one report: every SKU, every location, every Days of Stock score. Traffic light status — 🔴🟡🟢🔵. And one thing no spreadsheet can give you: "SKU X will stock out on June 18." Not a forecast. A date. Plus supplier reliability history — what they promised vs. what actually arrived.

**SC Delta**
Your sales plan is only useful if someone watches it every day. SC Delta does. The moment a SKU sells above plan — you get an alert, and SC Stock Radar recalculates its stockout date automatically.

## Layer 2 — Invoice Audit

**Badge:** Layer 2 — 3PL and Supplier Invoice Audit

### 3PL Invoice Audit: You're Paying Invoices Nobody Checked.

Your 3PL invoice has 40 columns. Hundreds of items. You have 3 minutes to review it. You approve it. Your supplier sent a PDF with updated pricing. You skimmed it. You approved it. SC Supplier Guard and SC 3PL Guard exist because that's how every brand operates — and that's exactly where the money goes.

**SC Supplier Guard**
Reads every supplier invoice in any format — PDF, Excel, email, image — and runs a 3-way match: invoice vs. contract price vs. PO quantity. Every discrepancy gets a severity rating. Every overcharge gets a draft dispute, ready to send. Before you pay.

**SC 3PL Guard**
Calculates the zone that should have been applied — warehouse ZIP to customer ZIP — and compares it to what was billed. FedEx Zone 6 charged. Zone 5 was correct. $3.90 per shipment. 17,500 shipments. Nobody noticed. Zone errors, wrong DIM weight, ineligible surcharges, wrong warehouse routing — caught line by line. Savings Report and dispute letter, ready to send. Before you approve payment.

Every invoice gets audited.
Every overcharge gets caught.
Every dispute gets sent.

## Layer 4 — Quality

**Badge:** Layer 4 — AI Quality Control & Inspection Management

### The Inspection Report Arrived. Is It in Your Inbox, Your Downloads, or a Shared Drive No One Updates?

**VERIX**
The moment the inspection report arrives by email — VERIX reads it, determines Pass, Fail, or Conditional, and acts instantly. Fail: manager alerted in chat, supplier draft ready to send. Pass: files sorted, report artifact created, result logged. Conditional: escalated with full details for the final call. Every inspection processed in seconds.

**SPECTRA**
Builds your complete product specification from everything you already have — existing docs, supplier emails, listing descriptions. Every product broken down component by component. Every finalized spec gets a unique number — referenced on every PO, every invoice, every inspection request. No more "we thought you meant the old spec."

**INSPEX**
Turns that spec into a precise, step-by-step inspection brief for the inspector. Component-by-component checklist. Clear Pass/Fail criteria per checkpoint. AQL sample size calculated automatically. The inspector arrives knowing exactly what to check — no phone calls, no guesswork, no missed defects.

One loop. Report → Spec → Brief.
Quality stops depending on who showed up that day.
