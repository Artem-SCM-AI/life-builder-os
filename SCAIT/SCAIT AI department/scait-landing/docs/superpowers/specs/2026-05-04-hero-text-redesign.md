**Date:** 2026-05-04

---

## Problem With Current Hero

Current H1: *"Your Supply Chain Is Leaking Money. SCAIT Catches It."*

Issues:
- Leads with Money — the lowest value tier. Stock is the #1 pain.
- Sub-headline is a feature list, not a value statement.
- Stats bar leads with $370K / invoice errors — financial audit framing undersells the product.
- "Leaking money" framing positions SCAIT as an audit tool, not an operating system.

---

## Value Hierarchy Rule (established this session)

When listing what SCAIT does, always order by business impact:

1. **Stock** — stockout prevention, overstock elimination, right product / right location / right price
2. **Operations / Fuel** — supplier communication, shipment tracking, quality control
3. **Money** — invoice auditing, financial recovery

Never lead with invoice recovery numbers as the primary hook.

### SC Supplier Guard
```
Reads every supplier invoice in any format — PDF, Excel, email, image — and runs
a 3-way match: invoice vs. contract price vs. PO quantity. Every discrepancy gets
a severity rating. Every overcharge gets a draft dispute, ready to send. Before you pay.
```

### SC 3PL Guard
```
Calculates the zone that should have been applied — warehouse ZIP to customer ZIP —
and compares it to what was billed. FedEx Zone 6 charged. Zone 4 was correct.
$3.90 per shipment. 17,500 shipments. Nobody noticed. Zone errors, wrong DIM weight,
ineligible surcharges, wrong warehouse routing — caught line by line.
Savings Report and dispute letter, ready to send. Before you approve payment.
```

### Closing
```
Every invoice gets audited.
Every overcharge gets caught.
Every dispute gets sent.

### VERIX
```
The moment the inspection report arrives by email — VERIX reads it, determines Pass,
Fail, or Conditional, and acts instantly. Fail: manager alerted in chat, supplier draft

Conditional: escalated with full details for the final call.

```

### SPECTRA
```
Builds your complete product specification from everything you already have —
existing docs, supplier emails, listing descriptions. Every product broken down

on every PO, every invoice, every inspection request.
No more "we thought you meant the old spec."
```

### INSPEX
```
Turns that spec into a precise, step-by-step inspection brief for the inspector.
Component-by-component checklist. Clear Pass/Fail criteria per checkpoint.
AQL sample size calculated automatically. The inspector arrives knowing exactly
what to check — no phone calls, no guesswork, no missed defects.
```

### Closing
```
One loop. Report → Spec → Brief.
Quality stops depending on who showed up that day.
