# Is Your Business Ready for AI?
### 25-Question Assessment

*Based on research from McKinsey, Gartner, MIT, and Cisco AI Readiness Index 2025–2026*

---

## Before You Start

Answer each question honestly — not how you'd like things to be, but how they actually are today.

**Scoring scale for each question:**
- **0** — Doesn't exist / we have no idea
- **1** — We've discussed it but nothing is in place
- **2** — Partially exists, inconsistently
- **3** — Exists and works, but has gaps
- **4** — Exists, systematic, measurable

## What the Numbers Say

> **80% of AI projects fail** to deliver intended business value *(Pertama Partners, 2026)*

> **95% of GenAI pilots** never reach production *(MIT State of AI in Business, 2025)*

> **84% of all failures** trace back to leadership problems — not technology

> Companies with **clear pre-approval metrics** succeed at 54% vs. 12% without them

> Companies with **sustained executive sponsorship** succeed at 68% vs. 11% when sponsorship lapses

> Successful projects invest **47% of budget in foundations** (data, governance, change management). Failed projects invest only 18%.

---

## The Three Highest-Leverage Actions

Regardless of your score, these three moves have the strongest impact on AI project success:

1. **Define one success metric before starting.** Not "improve efficiency" — "reduce order processing time from 4 hours to 45 minutes by Q3."

2. **Audit your data before building anything.** The #1 technical failure cause. Two weeks of honest data audit saves months of rework.

3. **Name one accountable person.** Not a committee. One person whose job it is to make this work.
